About IOLAB

At IOLAB Makerspace, we build bridges to innovation, we provide increasing access to designing, making, innovating and inventing through new tools and services for both creators and builder that makes it easy to turn a project into a product, and then into a company

IOLAB Makerspace is a place where people come together to learn, collaborate, invent, prototype and test new ideas using tools and equipment we provide them, that are normally unavailable to individuals.

Becoming a member of the iolab makerspace is pretty easy!
Just register and subscribe Then you have access to tools like:
CNC MillIng Machings,
3D Printers that prints faster and bigger,
CNC Lasser Cutters,
and a bunch of many more other Hand and Maching tools design to make your life easier as either a maker or a hacker.
