How to become a member

Becoming a member of IOLAB Makerspace costs only 7000 Naira per week. Just select the “Weekly Membership” from the Pricing cards below. You will then be redirected to a page with payment detail for weekly subscription

Monthly membership is available at a price rate of 30,000 Naira. That’s a whole month of access to our makerspace and tools! Just select the “Monthly Membership Plan” from the Pricing cards below. You will then be redirected to a page with payment detail for monthly subscription

Annual memberships are available at a discount rate of 335,000 Naira. That’s one whole month free! Just select the “Annual Membership Plan” from the Pricing Cards below. You will then be redirected to a page with payment detail for Annual subscription

Once we have received your first payment, signed membership agreement, signed liability waiver and emergency contact information, we will arrange a time to meet with you to give you access to the space.
